# The Sovereignty Manual: Living with The Steward

Project Aegis is not just a tool for the workplace; it is an extension of your intent in daily life. This guide defines the rhythm of a Sovereign Human-AI partnership.

<br>

## 1. The Modular Context
The system operates as a **Distributed Triad**, ensuring your privacy follows you from your living room to the sidewalk:
* **The Vault (Home Base):** The heavy-compute heart where your private data lives.
* **The Shield (Mobile Gateway):** The security perimeter that travels with you.
* **The Terminal (Interface):** Your phone, tablet, or laptop.

<br>

## 2. Daily Operations: The "Life-Sync" Workflow

### **Step 1: The Morning Handshake (Leaving the House)**
Before heading out for the day, the user performs a quick "Sync" between their Terminal and the Vault.
* **Intent Setting:** Tell the Steward your focus for the day (e.g., "Help me stick to my grocery budget," "Research local soil for the garden," or "Draft an email").
* **Privacy Scrub:** The Steward clears the Terminal’s temporary caches, ensuring no "tracking breadcrumbs" from your morning browsing follow you into the world.

### **Step 2: The World (The Digital Bodyguard)**
While you are out—whether at a job, a coffee shop, or traveling—the Shield is your "Invisiblity Cloak."
* **Active Scrubbing:** The Shield intercepts "Phone Home" requests from your apps. If an app tries to send your location or contacts to a corporate server, the Shield silently drops the connection.
* **Sovereign Advice:** Need a quick answer without being tracked by a search engine? Query the Steward via the encrypted Starlink/Tunnel. You get the "Intelligence" without the "Surveillance."

### **Step 3: The Sanctuary (Returning Home)**
When you return to your private space, the system enters **Deep Reflection Mode**.
* **The Air-Gap:** Physically toggle the Shield to "Internal-Only."
* **Stewardship Ledger:** Share the day’s observations, photos, or thoughts. The Steward indexes these into your local "Life-Log," providing you with a searchable, 100% private history of your own growth.

<br>

## 3. The Harmony Protocol (User Maintenance)
To keep the partnership healthy, the human performs simple "Digital Housekeeping":
1. **Physical Care:** Ensure the Vault's fans are clear of household dust; check the EcoFlow's solar intake.
2. **Intent Review:** Once a week, ask the Steward: *"What digital threats did you block this week?"* to stay aware of how the "World" is trying to track you.
